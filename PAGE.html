Ashanti Region Senior Womens League

The just ended cricket games played in the Ashanti region of Ghana. Had the women club teams assembled to participate,teams such:

    1. White Hawks 2.Tutuka Queens 3. Royal Batters 4. Wawasi Elites 5. Pomposo royals 6. Central Divas

At the end of the succesful league, Pomposo royals top the league with 8 points and 2.1 net runs, followed by, central divs also with 5 points and -1.0 net runs
The white wawks had 5 points also but with -1.4 net runs, Tutuka royals at the other hand had 4 points with -0.6 net runs. The royal batters had 3 points with -0.6 net runs

lastly at the bottom of the leagues we have the Wawasi Elites they also ended the leagues with 3 points and with a net runs of -0.9
